# Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** December 2, 2024

AutoAssist.ai ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website or use our services.

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide to Us

When you submit our "Free Homepage Preview" form or contact us, we collect:

- **Personal Information**: Full name, company name, email address, phone number (optional)
- **Project Information**: Website/Instagram handle, project goals, budget range
- **Consent Information**: Your consent to receive communications, timestamp, and IP address
- **Marketing Data**: UTM parameters and referring URLs

### Information Collected Automatically

- **Device Information**: Browser type, operating system, device type
- **Usage Data**: Pages visited, time spent on site, navigation paths
- **Technical Data**: IP address, referring URL, access times

### Cookies and Tracking

We use minimal cookies:

- **Essential Cookies**: Session management for form submissions
- **Analytics Cookies**: Privacy-first analytics (Plausible or Fathom) that don't collect personal data
- **Consent Cookies**: Store your cookie preferences

We do NOT use advertising or tracking cookies from third parties.

We use the information we collect to:

1. **Deliver Services**:

   - Communicate about your project
   - Provide technical support

2. **Improve Our Services**:
   - Analyze website usage and performance
   - Understand user needs and preferences
   - Develop new features and services

3. **Marketing Communications**:
   - Send occasional updates about our services (max 2 emails/month)
   - Share case studies and web design tips

4. **Legal Compliance**:
   - Maintain records for business purposes

We implement industry-standard security measures:

- **Encryption**: All data encrypted in transit (HTTPS/TLS) and at rest
- **Access Controls**: Restricted access to personal data
- **Secure Storage**: Database hosted on secure, compliant infrastructure
- **Regular Audits**: Periodic security reviews and updates

However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.
